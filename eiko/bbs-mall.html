BBS Mall

Eastleigh, Nairobi

Location:

Eastleigh, Nairobi

SIZE:

110,000M2

COMPLETED:

Architects’ of Record with Maestro Architects Ltd [MAL]

CLIENT:

Ten Commodities Ltd

SERVICE:

Architecture

Project Overview:Welcome to a new era of shopping, community, and entertainment in the heart of Eastleigh, Nairobi. BBS Mall is a groundbreaking development poised to redefine the retail landscape, offering a modern, vibrant, and inclusive shopping experience to the diverse and dynamic Eastleigh community.

Project Vision:The vision for BBS Mall is to create a dynamic, culturally rich, and economically thriving hub that caters to the needs and aspirations of Eastleigh residents and visitors. This development aspires to be more than just a shopping center; it is envisioned as a cultural and commercial cornerstone, fostering a sense of belonging and economic growth.

Key Features:

  1. Modern Retail Spaces: BBS Mall offers a diverse range of retail spaces, from fashion boutiques and electronics stores to culinary delights and specialty shops, ensuring a comprehensive shopping experience.
  2. Community Gathering: The mall features communal areas, courtyards, and open spaces designed for relaxation, socialization, and community events, reflecting the vibrant Eastleigh spirit.
  3. Entertainment and Leisure: A state-of-the-art cinema, gaming arcades, and family-friendly entertainment options make BBS Mall a destination for leisure and enjoyment for all ages.
  4. Culinary Delights: The mall boasts a diverse culinary scene, with a range of international and local eateries and cafes, offering delectable flavors to suit every palate.
  5. Cultural Fusion: BBS Mall celebrates Eastleigh's rich cultural diversity, providing spaces for cultural events, exhibitions, and festivals, fostering unity and understanding.

Project Milestones:

  1. Conceptualization: The project's conceptualization phase involved extensive market research, community engagement, and architectural design envisioning to ensure a mall that resonates with Eastleigh's unique culture and needs.
  2. Design and Development: This phase encompassed architectural planning, layout optimization, and integration of modern amenities, resulting in a comprehensive blueprint for construction.
  3. Construction and Fit-Out: During construction, modern infrastructure, sustainable practices, and aesthetic finishes were meticulously incorporated into the mall's structure.
  4. Tenant Onboarding: A diverse array of tenants, from local entrepreneurs to renowned brands, were onboarded, ensuring a diverse retail mix.
  5. Quality Assurance: Rigorous quality checks and safety measures were implemented to guarantee that BBS Mall meets the highest standards of construction and operation.
  6. Grand Opening: BBS Mall has officially opened its doors to the community, ushering in a new era of shopping, entertainment, and cultural celebration in Eastleigh.

BBS Mall is not just a shopping center; it's a celebration of community, culture, and commerce. It is a testament to the vitality and vibrancy of Eastleigh, Nairobi, and an embodiment of the limitless possibilities that arise when a community comes together to create something remarkable.
